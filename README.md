## Common Furnace Problems That Require Professional Repair

Furnaces are complex systems with multiple components working together. When one part fails, the entire system can be affected.

### Some of the most common furnace issues include:
- Furnace not turning on or shutting off unexpectedly  
- Weak or uneven heating throughout the building  
- Unusual noises such as banging, rattling, or screeching  
- Burning or musty odors coming from vents  
- Short cycling (frequent on-and-off operation)  
- Rising energy bills without increased usage  

Each of these problems signals the need for professional diagnosis and repair to prevent further damage.


## Signs You Should Schedule Furnace Repair Immediately

Waiting too long can turn a minor issue into a costly repair or full system replacement.

### Watch for these warning signs:
- Cold air blowing from vents  
- Thermostat not responding properly  
- Yellow or flickering pilot light instead of blue  
- Excessive dust or poor indoor air quality  
- Furnace running constantly without reaching desired temperature  

If you notice any of these issues, contacting a trusted local HVAC provider is the safest course of action.
